A smarter way to study for finals

This platform was built and curated by me with one clear goal: to make studying for finals as efficient as possible.

Over time, I have collected and organized more than 1,200 questions from finals, endblocks, and Moodle exams. Every question has gone through multiple rounds of review, including manual verification and AI-assisted rechecks, to ensure accuracy and relevance. The content will continue to grow, especially with the latest 2024–2025 finals.

Instead of spending hours on long materials, you go through concise, high-yield factual checks. Each point is paired directly with its answer, allowing you to review quickly and memorize efficiently. In most cases, a full final can be covered in around 30 pages.

Based on analysis of these 1,200 questions, around 89 questions have been repeated across different finals. The number is likely higher, as some appear in different wording. The pattern is clear: many exams rely heavily on repetition.

I hope this resource helps you save time and focus only on what actually matters.
